About me

Greetings! I'm Hunor Nagy, an aspiring web developer with a passion for crafting digital experiences. While I may not boast a lengthy professional background, my enthusiasm for learning and dedication to mastering the MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) stack have been the driving forces behind my journey.

My approach to web development is a fusion of creativity and technical proficiency. Through self-directed learning and hands-on projects, I've cultivated skills in building robust backend solutions with Node.js and Express.js, as well as creating engaging user interfaces with React.js.

Every project in my portfolio is a testament to my commitment to mastering the art of web development. I am excited about the endless possibilities this field offers and am eager to contribute my skills to innovative and collaborative projects.
